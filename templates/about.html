About

MealSwap is an application where Princeton students are able to explore other meal plan options on campus by requesting and fullfilling meal swaps. Our goal is to allow students to find other people from different meal plans to initiate meal exchanges with, get guest swipes, or simply have their late meal for the day.

To find out how to use Meal Swap to its fullest extent, please head to our tutorial page!
